# Post-event recap post

**Timing:** 1–2 days after the event.
**Goal:** thank everyone who made the event happen, celebrate the night, and keep
the community warm for the next meetup.

> ⚠️ **New template.** The source Notion guidance does not include a post-event
> recap format — this is built in the established ProductTank London brand voice.
> Refine it against your actual past recap posts when you have them to hand.

## Structure

1. Bold header line wrapped in 🙌 or 📣, e.g.
   `🙌 𝗧𝗵𝗮𝘁'𝘀 𝗮 𝘄𝗿𝗮𝗽 — 𝗧𝗵𝗮𝗻𝗸 𝘆𝗼𝘂, 𝗣𝗿𝗼𝗱𝘂𝗰𝘁𝗧𝗮𝗻𝗸 𝗟𝗼𝗻𝗱𝗼𝗻! 🙌`
2. A warm thank-you to attendees for coming.
3. Thank each speaker by name (tag them + their company) and nod to their talk.
4. Thank the MC/moderator (tag them).
5. Thank the sponsor (tag them).
6. A forward-looking line teasing the next event / inviting people to follow.

```
🙌 𝗧𝗵𝗮𝘁'𝘀 𝗮 𝘄𝗿𝗮𝗽 — 𝘁𝗵𝗮𝗻𝗸 𝘆𝗼𝘂, 𝗣𝗿𝗼𝗱𝘂𝗰𝘁𝗧𝗮𝗻𝗸 𝗟𝗼𝗻𝗱𝗼𝗻! 🙌

What a night! Thank you to everyone who joined us at <Venue> for an evening on
<theme>.

🎤 A huge thank you to our speakers:
• @Speaker 1 — <talk title>
• @Speaker 2 — <talk title>
• @Speaker 3 — <talk title>

see you at the next ProductTank London! 👋
```
