# Post-event recap post

**Timing:** 1–2 days after the event.
**Goal:** thank everyone who made the event happen, celebrate the night, and keep
the community warm for the next meetup.

Unlike the other post types, the recap is written as **one warm, flowing
paragraph** — not bullet points, and without a bold-Unicode header line. It opens
with 🎉, sprinkles in 🙌 / 💙, points people to a recording/recap link, and closes
with a row of hashtags.

## Structure (single paragraph)

1. Open with 🎉 and a "that's a wrap" line.
2. Thank the speakers by name (tag them) and what they spoke about.
3. Thank the sponsor (tag them) and the host/venue (tag them).
4. A line celebrating the community / the evening's energy, ending with 🙌.
5. CTA to catch up via the link for anyone who missed it, ending with 💙.
6. The catch-up link.
7. A row of hashtags.

## Template

```
🎉 That's a wrap on our latest ProductTank event! A huge thank you to @Speaker 1
and @Speaker 2 for sharing their experiences on <topic>. <One line of praise.>
Thanks also to our sponsor @Sponsor and host @Venue for making the evening
possible. Great conversations, thoughtful questions, and an energised product
community as always 🙌 Couldn't make it? Catch up via the link below and see you at
the next one! 💙 <catch-up link>
#ProductManagement #ProductTank #StartUp #ProductLeadership #ProductCulture
```

> Adjust the hashtags to the event's theme — swap in/out tags like `#StartUp` or
> `#ProductCulture` to match what the talks were about.
