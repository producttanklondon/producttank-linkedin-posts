# Announcement post

**Timing:** when the Meetup event is published.
**Goal:** introduce the event, the theme, the line-up, and drive RSVPs.

## Structure

1. Bold header line wrapped in 📣, e.g. `📣 𝗣𝗿𝗼𝗱𝘂𝗰𝘁𝗧𝗮𝗻𝗸 𝗟𝗼𝗻𝗱𝗼𝗻 𝗘𝘃𝗲𝗻𝘁 𝗔𝗻𝗻𝗼𝘂𝗻𝗰𝗲𝗺𝗲𝗻𝘁 📣`
2. A short paragraph on the event theme and what attendees will learn.
3. Venue, date, and the speaker line-up (tag each speaker + their company).
4. Thank the sponsor (tag them).
5. Name the MC/moderator (tag them + their role/company).
6. Detail block (date + time).
7. `RSVP now 👇` + `[meetup URL]`.

## Template

```
📣 𝗣𝗿𝗼𝗱𝘂𝗰𝘁𝗧𝗮𝗻𝗸 𝗟𝗼𝗻𝗱𝗼𝗻 𝗘𝘃𝗲𝗻𝘁 𝗔𝗻𝗻𝗼𝘂𝗻𝗰𝗲𝗺𝗲𝗻𝘁 📣

Join us for this month's ProductTank London, where we'll hear from <one-line theme>.

<1–2 sentences expanding on the theme and what attendees will take away.>

We will be at <Venue> on <Month Day> to learn from @Speaker 1, @Speaker 2, @Speaker 3, and @Speaker 4.

Thank you to our sponsor @Sponsor!

@MC Name — <role @ company> — will be our MC and moderator for the night.

🗓️ Date: <Month Day>
🕑 Time: 7 pm (UK Time)

RSVP now 👇
[meetup URL]
```
